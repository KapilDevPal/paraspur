Frequently Asked Questions

What is the tomato price today?

Today's tomato modal price in UP markets averages around ₹1,850 per quintal. Prices range from ₹1,600 (min) to ₹2,100 (max) depending on the arrival quantity and quality.

What is the MSP price of wheat?

The Minimum Support Price (MSP) for Wheat is fixed at ₹2,275 per quintal. Government procurement centers operate at this base rate.

How is the Mandi Modal Price calculated?

The Modal Price is the most frequently occurring rate at which the maximum quantity of a commodity is sold in the mandi on a given day. It gives a more accurate market trend than a simple average.
