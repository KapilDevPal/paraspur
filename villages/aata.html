Aata: A Flourishing Hamlet in the Heart of Gonda

A deep dive into the demographics, cultural roots, and evolving landscape of the Aata (Atta) village community.

Introduction to Aata Village

Aata, often spelled as Atta in official records, is a serene yet progressive village located in the Paraspur Block of the Gonda district, Uttar Pradesh. Situated in one of the most fertile belts of the Gangetic plains, Aata is a quintessential representation of the agrarian lifestyle of the Awadh region. The village is known for its peaceful environment and a community that has successfully combined traditional values with modern aspirations.

Population and Community

Aata is a medium-sized village with a robust population that plays an active role in the local economy. According to the 2011 Census data, the village has a balanced social structure.

2,398 Total Population
1,248 Males
1,150 Females

The village is home to approximately 450 households. One of the notable aspects of Aata's demographics is its healthy sex ratio of 921 females per 1000 males, which is higher than the state average of Uttar Pradesh.

Literacy and Education

Education is a core focus for the residents of Aata. The overall literacy rate stands at 58.42%, showing a positive trend towards better educational outcomes for the next generation.

Educational Statistics

  • Male Literacy: 69.45% — Reflecting a strong tradition of schooling among the male population.
  • Female Literacy: 46.52% — A figure that is rapidly growing as more girls enroll in local primary and middle schools.

The Agricultural Life

The land in and around Aata is extremely productive. The primary crops grown here include sugarcane, rice, wheat, and a variety of seasonal vegetables.

  • Farming Techniques: While many farmers still use traditional methods, there is an increasing adoption of modern equipment like tractors and efficient irrigation systems.
  • Local Livestock: Animal husbandry is a significant secondary source of income, with most households owning cattle for dairy production.

Governance and Rural Schemes

Aata is governed by a Gram Panchayat. The local leadership is dedicated to ensuring that the benefits of government programs reach every corner of the village. Residents actively participate in schemes such as:

PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
MNREGA Employment
Swachh Bharat Mission
Ayushman Bharat Cards

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the official Pin Code of Aata, Gonda?

The Pin Code for Aata village is 271504. It is served by the Paraspur sub-post office.

Are there schools in Aata?

Yes, Aata is served by government primary schools. For higher secondary education, students typically travel to nearby Paraspur town.

Which assembly constituency does Aata belong to?

The village falls under the Colonelganj Assembly constituency and the Gonda Lok Sabha constituency.

Summary

Aata is a village that prides itself on its agricultural heritage and its peaceful communal life. With its growing literacy and active participation in development schemes, it remains a vital part of the Paraspur block's rural landscape.
