Libraries in Paraspur, Gonda

छात्रों और पाठकों के लिए परसपुर के प्रमुख पुस्तकालय और डिजिटल स्टडी सेंटर।

Ambition Library

A modern study facility in Paraspur town, offering a dedicated environment for competitive exams and higher studies.

Guru Digital Library

Equipped with high-speed internet and digital research tools for modern students.

Dev Library

Popular reading room in the main market area, highly favored by local students for its quiet atmosphere.

Baba Darvesh Sarvajanik Pustakalaya

A public library providing access to a wide variety of literature and reference books for all age groups.

Other Notable Libraries

Sankalp Library, SHIVA LIBRARY, Rudra Digital Library, Om Library, Devansh Pustkalay, and Prabhat Library also serve the students of Paraspur block.
