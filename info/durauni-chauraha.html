Durauni Chauraha

The heartbeat of local commerce and transit in the Rajapur-Paska region.

Overview

Durauni Chauraha is more than just an intersection; it is the central nervous system for several villages in the Paraspur block of Gonda. Located strategically on the Durauni - Diwakar Nagar - Banuwa Road, it serves as the primary gateway for those traveling to the historic Goswami Tulsi Das Temple in Rajapur and the holy Paska Sangam.

Location Rajapur-Banuwa Road
Hub Type Commercial & Transit
Peak Hours 8 AM - 8 PM

Why Durauni Chauraha is Important?

For the people of Durauni village and surrounding hamlets like Pure Tiwari and Diwakar Nagar, this crossroads is the primary point for all their essential needs.

  • Transport Linkage: It is the main stop for e-rickshaws, autos, and private buses connecting Paraspur market to the river-side villages.
  • Economic Center: From mobile recharge shops to grocery stores and small eateries, the Chauraha is a thriving local market.
  • Pilgrimage Pathway: Thousands of pilgrims passing through on their way to Sukarkhet stop here for refreshments and local information.

Nearby Attractions

Tulsi Das Temple Just 2km away on the Rajapur road. Paska Sangam The holy confluence of Saryu & Ghaghara.

Road Connectivity

The Chauraha sits on the recently improved Durauni - Diwakar Nagar - Banuwa Road. This road has significantly reduced travel time for local residents and improved access to the Paraspur administrative headquarters.

Travel Tip: If you are traveling from Paraspur Market, follow the signs toward Paska. Durauni Chauraha is the major intersection you will encounter before reaching the Rajapur area.
