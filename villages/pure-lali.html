Pure Lali: A Thriving Community in Gonda

A deep dive into the demographics and unique social indicators of Pure Lali village.

Introduction to Pure Lali Village

Pure Lali is a small yet significant village situated in the Paraspur Block of Gonda district, Uttar Pradesh. Nestled within the fertile plains of the Gonda-Lucknow belt, this village represents the quintessential rural spirit of the Awadh region. Known for its strong agricultural roots and a tight-knit community, Pure Lali is a place where traditional values meet a growing aspiration for modern development.

Population & Demographics

Despite its relatively smaller geographical footprint compared to hubs like Paraspur, Pure Lali has a dense and active population. According to the 2011 Census, the village has made remarkable strides in its demographic stability.

2,595 Total Population
1,355 Males
1,240 Females

The village is composed of approximately 394 households. One of the most striking features of Pure Lali's demographics is its balanced sex ratio, which is higher than the state average for Uttar Pradesh.

Social Indicators: A Success Story

Pure Lali stands out in the region for its impressive sex ratio statistics. The average sex ratio of the village is 915, which is higher than the Uttar Pradesh state average of 912.

Impressive Child Sex Ratio

The child sex ratio (0-6 years) in Pure Lali is a remarkable 987, which is significantly higher than the Uttar Pradesh average of 902. This indicator reflects a positive social attitude towards girl children within the community.

Literacy & Education

Literacy is an evolving aspect of life in Pure Lali. The overall literacy rate stands at 57.77%.

  • Male Literacy: 67.99%
  • Female Literacy: 46.42%

While there is still work to be done to reach state and national averages, the village is seeing an increase in school enrollments. The proximity to educational institutions in the Paraspur block center allows students from Pure Lali to pursue higher secondary and degree courses with ease.

Economy & Lifestyle

The primary occupation in Pure Lali is agriculture. The village is surrounded by fertile land where crops like sugarcane, wheat, and paddy are grown in abundance. Many residents also work in nearby towns, contributing to a diverse local economy. The village architecture is a mix of traditional brick houses and modern concrete structures, reflecting the gradual transition of the area.

Local Governance

Pure Lali is administered by a Sarpanch (Village Head) who is elected through the Gram Panchayat system. The local administration focuses on maintaining village sanitation, implementing rural employment schemes like MGNREGA, and ensuring the smooth distribution of government welfare benefits.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Pin Code of Pure Lali, Gonda?

The official Pin Code for Pure Lali village is 271504. It falls under the Paraspur sub-post office.

How far is Pure Lali from Paraspur?

Pure Lali is located within the Paraspur Block and is well-connected to the main block headquarters by local roads, typically a short 10-15 minute drive.

What is unique about Pure Lali?

Pure Lali is notable for its exceptionally high child sex ratio of 987, which is one of the best in the Gonda district, indicating a progressive social environment.

Summary

Pure Lali is a village that exemplifies the positive changes occurring in rural Gonda. With its balanced demographics, growing focus on education, and strong community bonds, it remains an essential part of the Paraspur block's identity.
