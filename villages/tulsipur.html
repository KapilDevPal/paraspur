Tulsipur: A Peaceful Enclave in the Paraspur Block

Examining the demographics, local governance, and agricultural traditions of the Tulsipur village community.

Introduction to Tulsipur Village

The village of Tulsipur is a significant constituent of the Paraspur Block in Gonda district, Uttar Pradesh. Known for its quiet, pastoral charm and its productive farmland, Tulsipur represents the enduring agricultural spirit of the region. While it shares its name with larger towns in the neighboring districts, the Tulsipur of Paraspur has its own unique identity rooted in the local Awadhi culture and the close-knit social structure of its residents.

Population and Community Structure

Tulsipur is a medium-sized village that has maintained a steady demographic profile over the years. According to the 2011 Census of India, the village provides a home to a determined and hardworking community.

1,030 Total Population
542 Males
488 Females

The village consists of approximately 185 households. The population is characterized by a high degree of social cohesion, with residents actively participating in village festivals, agricultural collectives, and local governance meetings.

Literacy and Educational Foundations

Progress in literacy is a major goal for the Tulsipur community. The current literacy rate reflects the village's ongoing efforts to improve access to basic education.

  • Overall Literacy: 52.14%
  • Male Literacy: 64.12%
  • Female Literacy: 38.85%

While female literacy is an area that requires continued focus, the enrollment of young girls in the local primary school has seen a marked increase in recent years. The proximity to the educational resources of Paraspur and Gonda city is helping the youth of Tulsipur aspire to professional careers beyond the village.

Economic Life and Farming

Life in Tulsipur revolves around the seasonal cycles of agriculture. The soil here is particularly suited for high-yield crops, which form the backbone of the village economy.

  • Principal Crops: Wheat, Paddy (Rice), and Sugarcane are the primary focus of the farmers.
  • Self-Sufficiency: Many families also maintain small kitchen gardens and fruit trees, ensuring a steady supply of fresh produce for their own consumption.
  • Allied Activities: Dairy farming is a common practice, providing both nutrition and an additional stream of income for many households.

Welfare and Governance

Tulsipur is part of a Gram Panchayat that oversees its administrative needs. The local governance focuses on improving the quality of life through several state and central government initiatives:

PM Awas Yojana
MNREGA Labor Works
Ujjwala Yojana
Rural Electrification

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Where is Tulsipur village located?

Tulsipur is located in the Paraspur Block of Gonda district, Uttar Pradesh, falling under the Colonelganj Tehsil.

What is the Pin Code of Tulsipur, Gonda?

The official Pin Code for Tulsipur village (Paraspur) is 271504.

Which assembly constituency does it fall under?

The village is part of the Colonelganj Vidhan Sabha constituency.

Summary

Tulsipur is a village that exemplifies the quiet dignity of rural life in the Gonda district. With a strong commitment to its agricultural roots and a growing interest in education and modern infrastructure, it remains an integral part of the Paraspur block's community fabric.
