Abhayipur: A Progressive Gram Panchayat in Gonda

An analytical look at the demographics, education, and social structure of Abhayipur village.

Introduction to Abhayipur Village

Strategically located within the Paraspur Block of the Gonda district, Abhayipur is a village that balances traditional agricultural lifestyle with a steady drive towards modernization. As a designated Gram Panchayat, Abhayipur serves as a local administrative unit for several smaller hamlets nearby. Its placement within the Colonelganj tehsil makes it a vital part of the district's rural network.

Population & Demographics

Abhayipur has a well-defined community structure with a population that has shown resilience and growth. According to the 2011 Census of India, the village supports a diverse group of residents.

2,100 Total Population
1,139 Males
961 Females

The village is home to approximately 280 households. The population consists of various social groups, with the Scheduled Caste (SC) community making up about 22.95% of the total residents. This diversity contributes to a rich cultural tapestry within the Gram Panchayat.

Literacy & Educational Progress

One of the noteworthy features of Abhayipur is its literacy rate, which is competitive within the local block context. The overall literacy rate is 62.24%.

74.59%

While there is a significant gap between male and female literacy, the youth of Abhayipur are increasingly accessing educational facilities in the nearby Paraspur town and other neighboring centers. The village has its own primary and upper primary schools that serve as the foundation for the local children.

Economic Landscape

The economy of Abhayipur is rooted in agriculture. The fertile soil of the Gonda district allows for the cultivation of a variety of crops, with paddy, wheat, and pulses being the primary yield. A significant portion of the population (over 1,028 individuals) is actively engaged in work, either in farming or in small-scale local businesses. The proximity to the Gonda-Lucknow highway also facilitates the transport of agricultural goods to larger markets.

Governance & Infrastructure

As a Gram Panchayat, Abhayipur is governed by a Sarpanch and a team of elected members. The administration is focused on improving village roads, ensuring a steady water supply through the Jal Jeevan Mission, and implementing the various welfare programs of the state and central governments.

  • Governance: Gram Panchayat system with a Sarpanch.
  • Schemes: PM Awas Yojana, MNREGA, and Ayushman Bharat are actively utilized.
  • Post Office: The village falls under the Paraspur post office jurisdiction.

Summary

Abhayipur remains a key village in the Paraspur block, representing the growth and demographic diversity of the Gonda district. With a strong working population and a focus on improving literacy, the village is well-positioned for future development.
