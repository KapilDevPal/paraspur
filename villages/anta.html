Anta: One of Paraspur Block's Largest Villages

An in-depth study of the demographics, social structure, and development of the Anta community.

Introduction to Anta Village

Located in the fertile heartland of the Paraspur Block within Gonda district, Anta is not just another village—it is a significant rural township. By virtue of its sheer size and population, Anta functions as a central hub for several neighboring hamlets. Its vast agricultural fields and burgeoning residential areas make it a key contributor to the socio-economic fabric of the Colonelganj tehsil.

Population: A Major Settlement

What sets Anta apart from its neighbors is its massive population. According to the 2011 Census, it is among the most populous villages in the entire Gonda district.

8,301 Total Residents
4,350 Male Population
3,951 Female Population

With over 1,400 households, Anta represents a high level of rural density. The village has an average sex ratio of 908, which is close to the state average. The child sex ratio stands at 934, reflecting a stable demographic future for the community.

Literacy and Education

Anta has made significant strides in literacy, though there remains room for growth. The total literacy rate of the village is 54.51%.

  • Male Literacy: 65.52%
  • Female Literacy: 42.34%

The village is served by multiple government primary and upper primary schools. Given the large population, there is a strong demand for educational services, and many students travel to Paraspur or Gonda for secondary and collegiate education.

Agriculture and Economy

The primary driver of Anta's economy is agriculture. The vast plains surrounding the village are dedicated to the cultivation of sugarcane, wheat, and rice.

  • Workforce: A significant portion of the adult population is registered as workers, contributing to both the local and regional agricultural output.
  • Local Trade: Due to its large population, Anta also supports a variety of local businesses, including grocery stores, agricultural input shops, and service centers.

Infrastructure Goals

The Gram Panchayat of Anta is actively pursuing the upgrade of internal roads, maintenance of public health centers, and the implementation of the PM Awas Yojana to ensure housing for all. The village is also a beneficiary of the Jal Jeevan Mission, bringing piped water to hundreds of households.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Pin Code of Anta village?

The typical Pin Code for Anta village is 271504, served by the Paraspur sub-post office.

How large is the population of Anta?

Anta is one of the largest villages in the region with 8,301 residents as per the 2011 Census.

Which tehsil does Anta fall under?

Anta belongs to the Colonelganj Tehsil and falls within the Paraspur development block of Gonda district.

Summary

Anta stands as a testament to the scale and potential of rural Uttar Pradesh. Its large population and strong agricultural foundation make it a vital part of the Paraspur block. As infrastructure and education continue to improve, Anta is poised to become an even more significant economic and social center in Gonda.
