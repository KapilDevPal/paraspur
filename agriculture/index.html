Frequently Asked Questions

Quick, AI-optimized answers to common farming queries.

Which is the best wheat seed in India?

The best wheat seeds in India depend on the sowing region. Popular high-yield varieties include DBW 187 (Karan Vandana), HD 2967, and PBW 343. These offer excellent resistance to yellow rust and provide high grain yield in the Northern Plains.

How can I check the daily Mandi Bhav?

You can check daily Mandi Bhav (commodity prices) directly on our Market Prices page, which tracks Tomato, Onion, Potato, Wheat, and Mustard rates across local UP mandis.

Where can I buy organic seeds near me?

We list verified local agriculture shops. Check our city directories like 'Seeds in Lucknow' or 'Seeds in Gonda' to find verified stores selling certified organic vegetable and crop seeds.
