Bhauri Ganj: The Commercial Soul of Paraspur

Investigating the economic significance, demographics, and culture of the Bhauri Ganj trade center.

Introduction to Bhauri Ganj Village

Commonly known as Bhauriganj, this village is much more than just a residential settlement; it is a vital economic node in the Paraspur Block of Gonda district, Uttar Pradesh. The suffix 'Ganj' historically identifies it as a marketplace, a legacy that continues today as Bhauri Ganj serves as a primary trade hub for dozens of smaller hamlets in its vicinity.

Population & Demographics

Bhauri Ganj has always been a densely populated area due to its commercial activities. As per the 2011 Census of India, the village supports a large and active community.

4,194 Total Population
2,185 Males
2,009 Females

The village is composed of approximately 750+ households. The population is diverse, with a significant representation of various castes and religious groups who coexist and collaborate in the local marketplace.

The Economic Hub: Bhauriganj Bazar

The Bhauriganj Bazar is the heart of the village. It is a place where farmers from the surrounding countryside bring their produce—mostly wheat, paddy, and sugarcane—to sell to wholesalers.

  • Retail Activity: The market offers everything from electronics and clothing to essential groceries and agricultural seeds.
  • Weekly Markets: Special market days (locally called 'Haats') attract thousands of people, making it one of the busiest spots in the Paraspur block.
  • Employment: Aside from farming, a large section of the population is involved in retail trade, transport, and small-scale processing units.

Education & Infrastructure

Education in Bhauri Ganj has seen steady progress. The village hosts its own government schools and several private institutions that cater to the primary and middle education of the local children.

The connectivity of Bhauri Ganj is excellent, with well-paved roads linking it to the block headquarters in Paraspur and the tehsil in Colonelganj. Public transport in the form of buses and shared autos is frequently available, facilitating the daily movement of traders and students.

Welfare & Administration

Managed by a Gram Panchayat, Bhauri Ganj has been a model for implementing rural development schemes. The administration focuses on:

Market Sanitation
Solar Street Lighting
MNREGA Job Schemes
Jal Jeevan Mission

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Bhauri Ganj famous for?

Bhauri Ganj is famous for its large village market (Ganj/Bazar) which serves as a central trade point for agricultural produce in the southeast part of the Paraspur block.

What is the population of Bhauri Ganj?

According to the 2011 Census, the population of Bhauri Ganj village is 4,194 people.

Which post office serves Bhauri Ganj?

The village is served by the Paraspur sub-post office with the Pin Code 271504.

Summary

Bhauri Ganj continues to be a pillar of the local economy. Its transition from a traditional agrarian village to a bustling trade center is a testament to the enterprising spirit of its residents. For anyone looking to understand the rural economy of Gonda, Bhauri Ganj is a must-visit location.
