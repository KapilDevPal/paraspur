Andupur Village

Political & Administrative Details

Lok Sabha Constituency

Gonda Lok Sabha

Vidhan Sabha Constituency

Colonelganj Assembly

Governance: Managed by a Gram Panchayat with a Gram Pradhan (Village Head), Panchayat Members, and Village Secretary.

Economy & Agriculture

Andupur is primarily an agrarian village. Most residents depend on farming for their livelihood. The fertile land of Gonda district allows for the cultivation of various crops throughout the year.

Main Crops

Sugarcane, Wheat, Paddy (Rice)

Irrigation

Canals and Private Tube-wells

Markets

Paraspur & Colonelganj Mandi

Education & Infrastructure

Education is a growing priority in Andupur. While the village has primary educational facilities, students often travel to nearby Paraspur town for higher secondary and college education.

  • Primary Schools

    Government Primary and Middle Schools within the village vicinity.

  • Road Connectivity

    Connected via link roads to the main Gonda-Paraspur highway.

Government Schemes Available

Residents of Andupur benefit from several central and state-level welfare programs:

PM Awas Yojana
PM Kisan Samman Nidhi
Ayushman Bharat
MGNREGA

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Andupur village located?

Andupur village is located in Paraspur block of Gonda district in Uttar Pradesh.

Which assembly constituency covers Andupur?

The village falls under Colonelganj Vidhan Sabha constituency.
