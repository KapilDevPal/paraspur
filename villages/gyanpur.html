Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Gyanpur village located?

Gyanpur village is located in Paraspur block of Gonda district in Uttar Pradesh.

Which assembly constituency covers Gyanpur?

The village falls under Colonelganj Vidhan Sabha constituency.
