Paraspur: The Pulse of Gonda District

An in-depth guide to the administrative heart and largest settlement of the Paraspur block.

Introduction to Paraspur Village

Paraspur is not just a village; it is a thriving administrative hub and the namesake of the largest block in the Gonda district of Uttar Pradesh. Strategically located in the fertile plains of the Awadh region, Paraspur serves as a critical link between rural hamlets and the larger urban centers of Gonda and Lucknow. Known for its historical significance and its role as a center for education and trade, Paraspur is the economic engine of its block.

Population & Demographics

As per the 2011 Census, Paraspur village is home to a significant population, making it one of the most densely populated areas in the region.

13,644 Total Population
7,068 Males
6,576 Females

The literacy rate in Paraspur is impressive at 70.20%, which is higher than many surrounding rural areas. Male literacy stands at 77.92%, while female literacy is recorded at 61.81%. The community is diverse, with Scheduled Castes making up about 8% of the residents.

Educational Institutions

Paraspur has earned the reputation of being an 'Educational Hub' for the Western Gonda district. Students from over 90 villages travel to Paraspur for quality schooling and higher education. Key institutions include:

  • Tulsi Smarak Inter College: A landmark institution known for producing academic excellence for decades.
  • Maharshi Dayanand Saraswati Shikshan Sansthan: Focused on holistic development and modern curriculum.
  • Swami Leela Shah Convent Public School: A popular choice for English medium education.
  • Rosewood Inter College: Providing secondary education with modern facilities.

Healthcare & Medical Facilities

Residents of the Paraspur block rely heavily on the medical infrastructure available in the village. It hosts several critical healthcare units:

  • Community Health Centre (CHC) Paraspur: The primary government facility providing emergency care, vaccinations, and maternity services.
  • Khalilullah Hospital & Diagnostic Center: A private facility offering specialized diagnostic services.
  • Tiwari Hospital And Maternity Center: Known for child and maternal health services.
  • Maa Koteshwari Hospital: Serving the daily medical needs of the local population.

Location & Map

Paraspur is situated about 30 kilometers from the Gonda district headquarters and approximately 100 kilometers from the state capital, Lucknow. Its location on the main road makes it highly accessible via buses and private transport.

Governance & Welfare

The village is governed by a robust Gram Panchayat system. However, due to its size, Paraspur also functions with the characteristics of a semi-urban town (Nagar Panchayat). Residents are active participants in several government welfare schemes:

PM Awas Yojana
PM Kisan Nidhi
Ayushman Bharat
MGNREGA Registry

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the Pin Code of Paraspur Gonda?

The official Pin Code for Paraspur, Gonda is 271504. This code is used for all postal services in the Paraspur block.

Are there banks in Paraspur village?

Yes, Paraspur has several banking facilities including the State Bank of India (SBI), Allahabad Bank (now Indian Bank), and Gramin Banks, serving the financial needs of farmers and traders.

Which assembly seat does Paraspur fall under?

Paraspur is part of the Colonelganj Vidhan Sabha constituency and the Gonda Lok Sabha seat.

Summary

Paraspur remains the most vital location for anyone living in or visiting the western part of Gonda. With its strong educational base, comprehensive healthcare, and active marketplaces, it continues to lead the block's development into the 21st century.
