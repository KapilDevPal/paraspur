Mangura Bazar: THE Marketplace of North Paraspur

A comprehensive overview of the commercial importance and social life of the Mangura Bazar community.

Introduction to Mangura Bazar

Nestled in the northern reaches of the Paraspur Block in Gonda district, Mangura Bazar is a vibrant settlement that defines the intersection of agriculture and trade. While many villages in the region are purely residential, Mangura Bazar has evolved into a localized commercial center, providing essential goods and services to the surrounding rural population. Its name itself reflects its primary identity—a place of exchange and enterprise.

The Economic Heartbeat: Local Commerce

The defining feature of this village is the Bazar. Twice a week, the main thoroughfare of Mangura transforms into a bustling market where local artisans, farmers, and traders congregate.

  • Agriculture Trade: Farmers from nearby hamlets use Mangura Bazar as a collection point for seasonal crops like paddy and mustard.
  • Daily Essentials: The village permanent shops provide everything from construction materials to modern consumer electronics, reducing the need for residents to travel all the way to Gonda शहर for basic needs.
  • Services: The bazar is also home to several service providers, including mobile repair shops, tailoring units, and agricultural equipment rental centers.

Population & Social Structure

While specific census counts for "Mangura Bazar" often overlap with the broader Mangura Gram Panchayat, the area supports a diverse population of several thousand. The social fabric is closely knit, with most families having deep roots in the area spanning generations.

Community Characteristics

The community in Mangura Bazar is known for its entrepreneurial spirit. A high percentage of households are involved in some form of non-farm economic activity, alongside traditional agriculture.

Education & Youth

Mangura Bazar is focused on the future of its youth. The village is served by government primary and middle schools which focus on basic literacy and vocational skills. For higher secondary education (Inter College) and degree courses, students typically utilize the excellent road connectivity to travel to Paraspur or Aryanagar.

Governance & Rural Welfare

Administrative duties are carried out by the Mangura Gram Panchayat. Under the leadership of the Gram Pradhan, the village has seen improvements in various sectors:

Piped Water Supply
CC Road Construction
Individual Toilet Building
Ration Card Distribution

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the significance of the Bazar in Mangura?

The bazar is the primary commercial center for the northern Paraspur block, providing a platform for farmers to sell their produce and for residents to buy essential items.

Which block is Mangura Bazar located in?

Mangura Bazar is a key village and Gram Panchayat within the Paraspur Block of the Gonda district, Uttar Pradesh.

How is the connectivity to Mangura Bazar?

The village is well-connected by road to Paraspur and Gonda. Regular bus and taxi services are available for commuters.

Summary

Mangura Bazar stands as a shining example of how rural villages can transform into self-sustaining commercial hubs. With its active market, growing infrastructure, and motivated community, it continues to be a central part of the Paraspur block's developmental journey.
