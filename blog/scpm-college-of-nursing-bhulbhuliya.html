About SCPM College of Nursing

SCPM College of Nursing and Paramedical Science is a reputed private nursing and healthcare education institution situated at Haripur, Bhulbhuliya, Gonda, Uttar Pradesh. Established in 2008, it has grown to become one of the most recognized nursing colleges in the Gonda district, drawing students not only from Paraspur and Gonda, but from across Uttar Pradesh.

The college is approved by the Indian Nursing Council (INC) and the UP State Medical Faculty, and is affiliated with Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Avadh University (RMLAU) — giving its degrees nationally recognized value in the healthcare sector.

For residents and students of the Paraspur block, SCPM Bhulbhuliya represents a golden gateway into the nursing and paramedical professions — without having to travel to far-off cities like Lucknow or Varanasi.

Location & How to Reach

The college is located at Haripur Village, Bhulbhuliya, Gonda, Uttar Pradesh – 271313. Bhulbhuliya is a well-known locality in Gonda district and is easily accessible from:

From Paraspur

Approximately 15–20 km via Gonda-Bahraich Highway. Autos, shared jeeps, and local buses are available from Paraspur market.

From Gonda City

Around 8–12 km from Gonda city center towards Bhulbhuliya via the main highway. Regular city buses and tempos operate on this route.

Nearest Railway Station

Gonda Junction — well connected to Lucknow, Gorakhpur, and Delhi. From Gonda station, take a shared vehicle towards Bhulbhuliya.

Admission Process & How to Apply

Admission to SCPM College of Nursing is conducted in a transparent process aligned with state and national guidelines. Here's how it typically works:

1

B.Sc & M.Sc Nursing

Admissions are based on UP CNET (Common Nursing Entrance Test) conducted by Atal Bihari Vajpayee Medical University (ABVMU). Candidates are then allotted college seats through centralized counseling.

2

BPT & Paramedical Degree

Admissions via UP CPET (Common Paramedical Entrance Test), also conducted by ABVMU, followed by counseling rounds.

3

GNM & ANM Diplomas

Selection is merit-based on the qualifying examination. Candidates can apply directly to the college by paying an application fee (approx. ₹500) and submitting required documents.

4

Get the Prospectus

The college prospectus and application form can be obtained directly from the SCPM campus, Bhulbhuliya. Visit during office hours and carry your academic documents for reference.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is SCPM Nursing College INC approved?

Yes, SCPM College of Nursing is approved by the Indian Nursing Council (INC) and the UP State Medical Faculty, ensuring the nursing degree is nationally valid.

Which university is SCPM affiliated with?

The college is affiliated with Dr. Ram Manohar Lohia Avadh University (RMLAU), Faizabad, a recognized state university in Uttar Pradesh.

How do I get admission for B.Sc Nursing?

B.Sc Nursing admissions are through the UP CNET entrance exam conducted by ABVMU. After clearing the exam, seats are allotted via counseling. Visit the official ABVMU website for current schedules.

Is SCPM nursing college near Paraspur?

Yes! Located at Bhulbhuliya, Gonda, it is approximately 15–20 km from Paraspur — easily accessible via shared vehicles and local buses from Paraspur market.

What is the fee structure?

Fees vary by programme. Prospectus with detailed fee structure can be obtained from the college campus at Bhulbhuliya for approximately ₹500. Contact the admin office directly for current session fees.

Can boys also apply for nursing?

Yes, B.Sc Nursing and GNM programmes are open to male candidates as well, subject to INC regulations and counseling rules for the respective academic year.
