History of Paraspur

Paraspur, traditionally known as Parapur, is a historically significant block in the Gonda district of Uttar Pradesh. Rooted in the heart of the Avadh region, it has seen centuries of cultural evolution.

Ancient Roots

The region around Gonda was part of the ancient Kosala Kingdom. Paraspur's location near the Sarayu river basin made it a fertile ground for settlements through various dynasties.

Modern Era

Today, Paraspur is a bustling local hub for agriculture, education, and commerce in the western part of Gonda district.
