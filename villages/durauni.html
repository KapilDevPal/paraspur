Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Durauni village located?

Durauni village is located in Paraspur block of Gonda district in Uttar Pradesh.

Which assembly constituency covers Durauni?

The village falls under Colonelganj Vidhan Sabha constituency.

Durauni Chauraha: The Local Hub

Durauni Chauraha is one of the busiest and most important intersections in the Paraspur block. Situated on the Durauni - Diwakar Nagar - Banuwa Road, this crossroads serves as a vital transit point for residents traveling between Paraspur, Paska, and other neighboring regions.

01 Connectivity Hub

Connects Paska Sangam to the main road networks of Gonda district. It is a major stop for local transport including autos and private vehicles.

02 Local Commerce

The Chauraha features numerous shops providing daily essentials, hardware, and mobile services, making it a convenient shopping point for villagers.

For visitors heading to the Goswami Tulsi Das Temple in Rajapur, Durauni Chauraha is the primary milestone to keep in mind on the Banuwa Road.
